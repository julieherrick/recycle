About The Creators

We are a group of high school students who coded together at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. We decided to make this website our final project because we wanted to raise awareness on how to properly recycle and show that recycling does not have to be a boring task... instead it can be a fun one! We can prevent the world from getting polluted by paying attention to the little things.
